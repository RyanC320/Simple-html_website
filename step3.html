This step will ask you to cook steak with butter, rosemary, and peeled garlic

Follow the steps to get a medium steak:

  1. Heat up the pot and no oil needed

  2. Put steak inside, cook each side for 1 min

  3. Then put garlic, rosemary, and peeled garlic

  4. When butter is melted, use spoon to poor the butter on the steak

  5. Take three chopsticks and place on top of the empty bowl, place the steak on top of the chopsticks. Wait for 5 mins

  6. After all, cut the steak and put everything in the plate

  7. Enjoy Your Meal!!!
